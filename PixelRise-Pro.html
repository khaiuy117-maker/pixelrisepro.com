FAQ
Common questions
Is this really free? ▾
Yes, completely free. No account, no credit card, no watermark, no daily limit. Processing happens in your browser using the Canvas API.
Are my images uploaded anywhere? ▾
Never. PixelRise Pro is a fully client-side tool. Your image data never leaves your device — all computation happens locally in JavaScript via the Web Canvas API.
How long does 8K or 16K take? ▾
It depends on your CPU and the source image size. 2K and 4K complete in seconds. 8K may take 30–120 seconds. 16K can take several minutes and requires significant RAM. Modern machines handle it; older ones may struggle with very large sources.
What algorithm does it use? ▾
A three-stage pipeline: (1) Lanczos-3 resampling for the base upscale, (2) frequency separation to independently enhance tonal and detail layers, and (3) adaptive unsharp masking targeting edge regions detected by luminance gradient analysis.
What file formats are supported? ▾
Any format your browser can decode — typically JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, AVIF, and BMP. Output is always PNG for maximum quality.
Can I use the output commercially? ▾
Yes. PixelRise Pro places no restrictions on your output. You retain all rights to your images. Just ensure you have the rights to the original source image.
